01

Wingless Dreamer

SEO Optimisation Content Strategy Website Management Internal Linking
#1 Google Ranking
1 Month to Rank
Online Publishing
Read Case Study

Client Background

Small online publishing firm helping first-time writers publish poetry and short stories through anthologies. Also sells notebooks and journals. Website hosted on Wix.

Challenge

Website was not SEO-optimised. No keywords on pages, no meta descriptions, broken internal linking, incorrect tagging, heavy images slowing load speed, content published on a whim with no strategy.

Strategy

Intensive keyword research for all web pages, full on-page SEO optimisation, reduced image/video file sizes for faster loading, built a topical authority content strategy, created blog posts with deliberate internal linking to store, case study, project, and testimonials pages, wrote content briefs for teammates, edited all submissions for SEO compliance, ensured consistent weekly publication schedule.

Results

Blog content published consistently every week without gaps. One blog post ranked on Google Page 1 for its target keyword within one month. Transformed the site from random publishing to a research-backed, strategically linked content ecosystem.

02

Lagoon Water: Sip for Change

Campaign Strategy VSL Script Landing Page Copy Social Media Banner Ads
High Visibility & Engagement
Top 4% HNG Finalist Project
FMCG / Campaign
Read Case Study

Client Background

Flavoured drinking water sourced from Lagos Lagoon, Nigeria. Inherent trust problem — Lagos Lagoon is known as a polluted body of water. Product also has different flavours.

Challenge

Create an entire marketing campaign for a product consumers would instinctively distrust. Deliverables included full campaign strategy, VSL script, social media copy, website/landing page copy, tagline, and unique campaign angle.

Strategy

Defined a specific ICP — eco-conscious, socially aware consumers motivated by giving back. Did intensive product research to turn features into meaningful benefits. Created a unique angle that reframed buying the water from a transaction into participation in a social movement. The angle: a percentage of every purchase goes toward cleaning the Lagos Lagoon, restoring marine life, and supporting 1,800+ fishing families whose livelihoods depend on it.

Results

Campaign received significant visibility, engagement, and approval. Chioma was an HNG Internship 12 finalist for this project — placing in the top 432 out of 11,285 participants.

NoCode SaaS Agency

Client Background

SaaS development agency startup. No LinkedIn presence, needed to build visibility from scratch and generate their first clients through LinkedIn.

Strategy

Two-pronged approach. Inbound — LinkedIn profile optimisation, consistent articles and lead magnets to build trust and generate inbound leads. Cold outreach — used LinkedIn Sales Navigator with precise ICP filters, sent high-volume but highly targeted connection requests, researched every prospect individually before messaging, sent personalised human-first messages, built rapport and asked qualifying questions before any pitch, used every conversation to refine targeting.

Results

Increased the startup's LinkedIn visibility by 3x compared to competitors within weeks. Achieved 30–50% reply rate on cold outreach. Booked approximately 5 sales calls. Achieved approximately 10% acceptance rate for case studies. Generated multiple positive future-build conversations.
